5.2.2 Alternative Form

Once we see some signals, before we cahnge or morph it too far away, we should know their siblings. No inferior than these presented way. Or they are just a little bit diffs some are more simple than another but not too much.

同义形式举例：

  • -\ln{x} = \ln{\dfrac{1}{x}}
  • 2\log_2{3} = \log_2{9}=\log_{\sqrt{2}}{9}
  • a^2-b^2 = (a+b)(a-b)
