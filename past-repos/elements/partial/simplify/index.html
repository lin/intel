5.2.2 Alternative Form

We prefer short forms. In that case we can remember less. So simplied forms EXPOSE MORE TOOLS for you. It opens ways or paths for you. It also REDUCE THE COMPLEXITY you need less calculation. you care less variables. less conditions.

化简举例

  • x\ln{x} \ge x^2 -x \Rightarrow \ln{x} \ge x -1
  • simplify reduce the computation at each turn early simplication can make work much easy to compute.
  • We can use more tools if we have a simplier form. since the majority of our toolbox is for simple forms
  • along the way, if the desired form is very closed to what we have in hand, we should directly transfer to that form.
  • Simplication is the midpoint (stepping stone)
